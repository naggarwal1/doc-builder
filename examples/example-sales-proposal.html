01 · Summary

Summary

Northwind Health's member portal is the front door to your entire product experience. Members use it to enroll, manage dependents, submit claims, and resolve issues. Small frictions there become large drops in retention and support load.

We'd like to partner with you on a focused 3-month engagement to rebuild the portal from the ground up. The work breaks into three phases: discovery & research, design system and high-fidelity mockups, and engineering handoff with QA support. By the end, you'll have a production-ready design, a documented system your engineering team can extend, and the research artifacts to justify future decisions.

We've done this kind of work with three healthtech companies in the last 18 months. The playbook is tested. The timeline is realistic.

02 · Scope

Scope

Phase 1: Discovery & Research (4 weeks)

  • Stakeholder interviews across product, engineering, support, and clinical teams (estimated 12-15 conversations)
  • Member research: 20 moderated sessions, 200 survey responses, support-ticket pattern analysis
  • Competitive and analogous-experience audit across healthcare, insurance, and adjacent consumer fintech
  • Findings report with prioritized opportunity map

Phase 2: Design System + Mockups (5 weeks)

  • Design system built in Figma: type scale, color tokens, spacing grid, component library (est. 60-80 components)
  • Accessibility guidelines baked in at the token level (WCAG 2.2 AA minimum)
  • High-fidelity mockups for 12 priority member flows
  • Two rounds of review and iteration with your team

Phase 3: Engineering Handoff + QA Support (3 weeks)

  • Full design specifications documented alongside each component
  • Kickoff workshop with your engineering team (half-day, on-site preferred)
  • Weekly office hours for the engineering team during build-out
  • Design QA on 3 major release candidates
